# Malpractice Prevention

## Introduction
We have already witnessed the rapid evolution of AI and its tools, which has not prevented malicious actors from using this new technology in a harmful way. Even the internet is full of such actions of abuse, defamation, and blackmailing. Anyone can download our pictures, voices, and videos in order to defame our reputations and cause irreparable harm to our character, or for blackmail purposes. 

Following precautionary steps can be great, but regardless, our information is already out there thanks to social media and other platforms. I believe everyone has the right to prevent any person or entity from using their data and signature, because privacy is a right, not a request. 

Driven by this ambition, I am starting a worldwide community that is willing to protect ourselves and our beloved ones from bad actors, and from the misuse of our pictures, audio, and videos. The main target for this movement is to protect women from the abuse of their data, to prevent pedophilia and blackmailing in general, and to stop digital bullying, as it not only harms victims but also is the cause of many suicides among teenagers.

## The First Stage
* We are more than capable of creating a digital entity-print to prove the ownership of facial characteristics and voice. 
* Connecting them to a person who can register them to prevent their misuse.

## The Second Stage
Forcing companies and large entities to implement these new authentication and verification technologies. This step is to prevent the following:
* **Prevent Catfishing:** By preventing the uploading and using of profile photos that do not belong to the users themselves inside applications. This step will significantly reduce the number of scams that people are facing.
* **Deepfake Prevention:** Prevent AI tools from receiving and modifying pictures that do not belong to the user themselves by adding a facial verification step before providing the processed image. The goal of this step is to prevent bad actors from misusing other people's photos; this will stop pedophiles and bad actors who are misusing women's pictures.

## The Third Stage
* Having the ability to publish our photos and videos without having the fear of them being downloaded or misused by others, by preventing the download of these photos and videos.
* Being able to report and remove biometric information, pictures, or voices that do not belong to the actor.

## The Fourth Stage
* **Global recognition:** by adopting this technology across government and global companies and connecting it to personal identity, it protects against its misuse.

## The Fifth Stage
* Preventing and prohibiting the taking of pictures or recording of voice without the consent of the other parties.
* By enforcing these rules on smartphones and devices.
